### Sublime Grow Systems is a hardware / software project with the mission to "demystify and democratize plant care systems and knowledge."

Plants are one of our most fundamental resources. Food, medicine, textiles, building materials, ecological benefits that most of us take for granted. Yet the tools and knowledge needed to grow them confidently have never been more opaque.
Most commercial solutions just ask you to trust them. We think you deserve to understand how your system works and how it reaches its conclusions.
Sublime Grow Systems builds open-source hardware and an open-core software platform so that hobbyists, communities, researchers, and professionals alike can do exactly that. No black boxes. No "just trust us and buy our solution." Just tools that are transparent by design, so you can grow with confidence even without years of experience.
